Couch Pillows

Couch pillows are small, decorative soft furnishing items made from a wide range of textiles including cotton, linen, silk, leather, microfibre, suede, chenille, and velvet. Couch pillows are usually (loosely) placed on sofas or armchairs but are also frequently used on beds, day beds and floors.
